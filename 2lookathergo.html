Look At Her Go

Look At Her Go is a university project by a BA(HONS) Music Journalism student, Maria Galea.

This website hopes to shine a light on the many women who make up the music industry, as well as getting to know more the past, present, and future state of women in music.

I was approached to create the identity and digital illustrations associated with the blog.
